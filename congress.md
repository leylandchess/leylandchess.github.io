Leyland hosts two lively chess congresses each year
- Standard play on August Bank Holiday weekend
- Rapid play on a Sunday in the second half of February

The venue is
The Business Centre, South Ribble Borough Council Civic Centre,
Leyland, PR25 1DH.

Details of the August 2026 congress will be posted here when available.
